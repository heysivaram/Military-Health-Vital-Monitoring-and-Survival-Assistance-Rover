# Project Working

Step 1

The soldier wears the health monitoring band which continuously measures vital parameters.

Step 2

The band collects data from the heart rate sensor and temperature sensor.

Step 3

The rover moves in the battlefield environment and scans for obstacles using an ultrasonic sensor.

Step 4

Environmental sensors detect hazards such as gas leaks or fire.

Step 5

If abnormal health conditions or environmental hazards are detected, alerts are triggered.

Step 6

GPS location data is transmitted to the command center for monitoring and rescue operations.
