# System Architecture

The system consists of two subsystems.

## Wearable Health Monitoring Band

The wearable band measures vital parameters of the soldier including heart rate, blood oxygen level and body temperature.

These measurements are transmitted to the rover or monitoring system.

## Surveillance Rover

The rover performs environmental monitoring and battlefield exploration.

The rover integrates multiple sensors to detect hazards and threats.

The rover can detect:

Obstacle  
Human movement  
Gas leakage  
Fire hazards  

The rover also transmits GPS coordinates for tracking soldier location.
